Coyocán

Coyocán is the artistic region of Mexico City. Here you'll find a lot of street art, museums, students, and vibrant culture. It's a great place to explore to get a different experience.

Activities

La Cineteca Nacional

If you're going to see a movie in Mexico City, see it here! This is a really cool theater, playing independent films in Spanish and English. The sound and picture quality is fantastic and the line-up as well. For a tourist the prices are also very cheap!

The Blue House - Frida Kahlo Museum

The Blue House is probably the most intimate Frida Museum in Mexico City. The house is filled with artifacts from her personal life, and dives into the relationship with herself and Diego Rivera (the famous Mexican muralist whose work can be found all over the city). It's a very interesting look into both of their lives and a must-see if you're in Coyocán. Check out the official website here .

Food

Mercado de Coyocán

The market itself is a main attraction in Coyocán. It is large in size and filled with all kinds of interesting fresh and cooked food. It's probably the best place to eat in the area, and right next to the Frida Kahlo Museum. Have cash in hand, as many places don't accept credit cards. Click here to go to their Yelp review location.

La Barraca Valenciana

La Barraca Valenciana is known for their world-class tortas, among other things. It's also a rare treat, as it serves Spanish food in Mexico. They also have excellent tapas. This is a great choice for a meal, and also close to the Frida Kahlo Museum. Click here to go to their reviews and get the location.
