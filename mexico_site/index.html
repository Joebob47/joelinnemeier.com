Purpose of This Site

This website is intended as a travel guide for newcomers to Mexico City. The enormity of the city in size and options can be overwhelming for tourists just arriving. I spent 5 months living in the city, and have chosen my 6 favorite neighborhoods. Each page will direct you to information on my favorite food and activities within each region. This site could easily be used as trip itinerary if you have say, a week to spare. Please enjoy and reach out for any questions!
