Jica's Stomping Grounds

Jica has found her permanent home with us. Although we're prone to moving, she'll always be with us wherever we go. Currently her habitat is in downtown Santa Rosa, where our apartment is.
    She likes to roam the streets on leash, hike in places like Taylor Mountain, and roll around in all forms of grass and bush. But she can only really be herself off leash, like when she's chasing a frisbee in an open field.
